Handle Carefully

Cake Transport Guide .

Ensure safe transport by keeping the cake box level and maintaining a cool environment. Follow these steps for a perfect delivery.

How to carry the cake box?

Carry the cake box carefully with both hands- one hand at the bottom and one on a side. The box must always be kept straight & not tilted at all.

Where to place the box?

Place the box on the floor of the car (best place to keep it is on the floor of front row passenger side).

Should the car AC be on?

AC in car must be on at all times. Drive very carefully- avoid potholes, sudden breaks, accelerations & sharp turns!

How to bring the cake out?

To bring the cake out, carefully cut box’s sides open using a sharp knife & gently slide the cake out.
